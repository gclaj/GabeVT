Gabe believes voters deserve to see a candidate’s thinking in full — published, signed, and open to challenge. Here is his recent writing and campaign coverage.

VTDigger · Op-Ed

Vermont’s future depends on restoring legal immigration

May 14, 2026

As federal restrictions tighten, Vermont is losing the workers, students, and neighbors it needs. Gabe lays out a state-level agenda: restore legal pathways, support refugees and students, build housing for newcomers, and partner with employers who rely on global talent.

VTDigger · Op-Ed

The lesson of Act 181 is that trust matters

April 2, 2026

Act 181 revealed a fracture — and an opportunity. Gabe argues Vermont cannot build durable environmental policy without the trust of the people who care for most of the land, and calls for humility, listening, and common sense over ideology.

VTDigger · Op-Ed

Update CHIP to build flood-ready housing

March 6, 2026

The places with water, sewer, and walkability are often the same places shaped by the floodplain. Gabe proposes a targeted, fiscally conservative fix: let CHIP reimburse engineered, flood-resilient lower levels so Vermont can build housing and resilience at once.

June 2026

The core drivers of Vermont’s affordability crisis remain untouched. Gabe makes the case for a long-term strategy that tackles housing supply, healthcare inflation, and the tax burden on year-round residents — together.
